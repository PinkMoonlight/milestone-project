about me

My name is Kate. My journey into tech started initially with writing about my travel adventures. I wanted a place to keep record of my travels, not even expecting people other then family and friends to read it. When I learned how to build my first website, I realised I found something that gave me the freedom & versatility I was looking for in my work. Something as well, that could build on my Bachelor of Arts in Film and Screen Production and channel my creativity, organisation skills and eye for detail in a new direction. Now I've made a full switch to front-end development.

I am an Australian living in the Silicon Valley Hub of California, and as a former Travel Agent, organisation and attention to detail are seared into my core being.

Can't wait to work with you!
